Our very first build wasn't for a client. It was for Vanessa's daughter, who has sensory needs around food. A small, gentle iOS app called Can Eat, Will Eat to make everyday eating decisions easier, and the test that proved a heartfelt idea can ship fast, and beautifully. It's still live on the App Store today.
