Crossways

A private app for the people of one estate, quietly keeping a whole community on the same page.

CLIENT
Crossways estate
SCOPE
Design + native build
ACCESS
Private / residents only

One estate. A dozen tiny frustrations.

Shared bins, shared spaces, shared questions, and no good place to sort any of it. Crossways replaces the group chats, paper notices and "did anyone put the bins out?" with a single calm app made just for the people who live there.

Built natively for iOS and Android, distributed privately to residents only.

Everything the estate needs, nothing it doesn't.

Bin-day reminders

Never miss a collection again. Clear, scheduled nudges so the right bins go out on the right night.

Shared-space booking

Reserve communal areas and facilities in a tap, no clashes, no double-booking, no awkward notes.

Neighbour noticeboard

A friendly place to ask, offer and announce, the village hall pinboard, in everyone's pocket.

Estate updates

Important messages reach every resident directly, so nothing gets lost in a thread or left on a door.

Designed to feel like home.

Design

A warm, low-friction interface that any resident, of any age, can pick up without a tutorial.

Native build

Built natively and written in-house for genuine speed and reliability on both iOS and Android.

Private rollout

A closed distribution so the app stays for residents only, private by design.
